# Privacy Policy — ReefScribe

**Effective date:** February 7, 2026  
ReefScribe is published by **PikeView Digital**.

ReefScribe is a mobile-first, offline-first aquarium journal. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Summary
- ReefScribe stores your aquarium data **locally on your device** (SQLite database).
- Photos you add are stored **locally on your device** in an app-managed folder.
- ReefScribe can create **backup ZIP files** that include your local database and photos. Backups are saved to a user-visible folder on your device (for example: `Documents/ReefScribe/Backups`).
- ReefScribe does **not** run its own servers and does **not** collect analytics or crash reports.
- ReefScribe uses **Google Play Billing** to process one-time purchases for the Pro/lifetime unlock.
- ReefScribe can use **local notifications** for reminders.

## Information ReefScribe handles

### Data you enter
ReefScribe stores information you choose to enter, such as:
- Tanks (name, volume, type, optional photo)
- Water changes (date, volume, notes)
- Parameter measurements (values and timestamps, ranges you set)
- Inhabitants (with optional photos and lifecycle status/history)
- Notes (title/body, type, pinned status)
- Reminder settings (schedule and notification preferences)

This information is stored locally on your device.

### Photos
If you add photos, ReefScribe stores them locally in an app-managed folder on your device.

### Backups
When you export a backup, ReefScribe creates a ZIP file containing your local database and photos and saves it to a user-visible folder on your device. You can share or move this file using your device’s share options.

## Data collection, sharing, and selling
ReefScribe:
- **Does not collect** personal data to PikeView Digital servers.
- **Does not share** your personal data with third parties for advertising.
- **Does not sell** your personal data.

## Purchases
If you purchase the Pro/lifetime unlock, the transaction is processed by **Google Play** via Google Play Billing. PikeView Digital does not receive or store your full payment card details. Purchase processing is subject to Google’s policies and terms.

## Notifications
If you enable reminders, ReefScribe may request permission to send **notifications**. Notifications are generated locally on your device.

## Security
ReefScribe stores your data on your device. You are responsible for protecting access to your device and any backup files you export or share.

## Children’s privacy
ReefScribe is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy
This policy may be updated over time. The effective date at the top will be updated when changes are made.
