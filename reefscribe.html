About ReefScribe

ReefScribe is an offline-first aquarium log app designed for reef keepers and aquarium hobbyists. Track water parameters, log maintenance, manage livestock, and keep detailed notes — all stored locally on your device.

View on Google Play (Coming Soon)

Key Features

  • Track water parameters with charts and trend analysis
  • Log water changes and maintenance
  • Manage fish, coral, and invertebrates with photos
  • Set reminders for testing and routine tasks
  • Detailed notes and observation logging
  • Offline-first with local backups
  • No accounts, no ads, no subscriptions

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. ReefScribe does not collect personal data, does not use analytics, and does not require an account.
